Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our packaging, sourcing, shelf life, and regional delivery details.

Are Vamika Spices 100% natural?

Yes, all our whole spices (Jeera, Ajwain, Dhaniya) are 100% pure and natural. We do not add any artificial coloring, artificial preservatives, additives, or chemical polishers.

How do you ensure spice hygiene?

Our sorting and packaging facilities are thoroughly sanitized daily. Workers follow strict protocols including head covers, gloves, and sanitization before sorting. Every batch is sealed in high-grade food-secure packaging to keep moisture and dust completely out.

Do you accept bulk orders for shops?
